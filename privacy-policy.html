🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 28, 2026

Magic Solo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to providing a safe, secure, and educational experience for children. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information and how we protect children's privacy in compliance with:

  • Google Play Families Policy
  • Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
  • GDPR-K (General Data Protection Regulation for Kids)

By using Magic Solo, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Children's Safety Commitment

Magic Solo is a child-directed educational application created specifically for children and families.

We prioritize:
  • Child safety
  • Privacy protection
  • Secure learning experiences
  • Age-appropriate content
Magic Solo does NOT include:
  • Public chat systems
  • Messaging features
  • Social media integration
  • User-to-user communication
  • Public profile sharing

Children cannot communicate with strangers through the app.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We follow a strict Data Minimization Policy. We do NOT collect, store, or share:

  • Full names or Phone numbers
  • Email addresses from children
  • Home addresses or Real-time GPS location
  • Contacts or Biometric information
  • Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
Users can use the app without:
  • Registration or Login
  • Creating an account

3. Local Data Storage

Magic Solo stores learning progress (completed levels, scores) only on the local device using secure local storage (Hive). This data is:

  • NOT uploaded to any server
  • NOT shared with third parties
  • Automatically removed when the app is uninstalled

4. Internet Access & Third-Party Services

Internet access is used only for essential app functionality, child-safe video content, and anonymous analytics. We only use third-party services that comply with Google Play Families Policy.

A. Google Play Services

Used for secure app distribution and essential Android services.

B. YouTube Player (Video Content)

Some educational content (stories and rhymes) may be displayed via YouTube's embedded player.

  • We only embed pre-selected, age-appropriate, child-safe videos
  • YouTube's own privacy policy applies to video playback
  • No YouTube account or login is required to watch videos in the app

C. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

Magic Solo uses the device's built-in Text-to-Speech engine to read stories and alphabets aloud. This feature works entirely on-device. No audio data is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

D. External Links

Certain sections of the app may use a URL Launcher to open external links (such as this Privacy Policy page) in the device's browser. We only link to our own official pages.

5. No Data Selling

We do NOT sell, rent, or share children's personal information with third parties.

6. No Social Features

Magic Solo does not contain chat rooms, messaging systems, or social networking features. Users cannot interact with strangers inside the app.

7. Security Measures

We implement reasonable safeguards to prevent unauthorized access and maintain a secure user experience. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

8. Parents' Rights

Parents and guardians have the right to review our privacy practices and contact us regarding safety concerns. If you believe we have unintentionally collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. COPPA Compliance

Magic Solo complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do NOT knowingly collect, use, or share personal information from children under 13 years of age.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any updates will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
